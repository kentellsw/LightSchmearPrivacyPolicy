# Light Painting Privacy Policy
### Policy
1. The app needs access to the camera to fulfill its main purpose (taking photos).
2. The app needs access to the device storage in order to save the photos you take.
3. The app only accesses (reads and writes) files in the directory you tell the app to save your photos to.
Those directories could be /DCIM, /DCIM/100_CFV5, or any other directory in case you instruct the app to save your photos to another, custom location.
4. The app also saves settings and other internal configurations to the private portion of the internal storage Android dedicates to each app, and for which apps do not need any permission to write to as it storage private to the app.
5. The app offers you the possibility to add geolocation information to your photos by embedding your device position into photos’ metadata. To do this, a combination of GPS information and cellular location is employed.
This is optional, and disabled by default.
6. The app uses the Fused Location Provider from Google Mobile Services, and therefore this feature is subject to its privacy policy.
If geolocation is enabled on the app, the location of your device is not shared by the app in any way, unless you decide to actively share photos containing location information with third party services, in which case the location information (if it is present) is shared along with the image and then you are subject to the privacy policy and terms of service of those third party services.
We (the developers of the app) do not collect your location.
7. The app is offered for a low price on the Google Play Store.
8. The app might collect anonymous usage statistics and error reports.
That information is completely anonymous and do not contain any user-identifiable data. Among others, the statistics and reports contain internal camera parameters and error information like the position of the program where the error happened. That information help us fix issues faster and improve the quality of the app.
In a case of an error in the app we collect data and information (through third party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when it was being used, the time and date of the event, and other statistics.
For users outside the European Economic Area, the data collection can be disabled by opting out in Settings/About tab/Privacy.
For users in the European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) require us to gather explicit consent from you to enable any kind of data collection, and therefore all data collection is disabled by default. You can opt-in to the anonymous statistics and error reports in Settings/About tab/Privacy.
9.The app does not use cookies explicitly (“cookies” are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers). However, the app may use third party code and libraries that use cookies to collect information and improve their services. If you do not accept the usage of cookies, you need to opt-out of statistics and error reports entirely.
